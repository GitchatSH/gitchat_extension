Trade-offs

A) Accordion

  • Consistent with Discover tab
  • Collapse "Not on GitChat" to focus on active friends
  • Count badges visible when collapsed
  • More structured, native VS Code feel

B) Flat list

  • Simpler, Telegram/Discord-like
  • Faster to scan (no click to expand)
  • Natural scroll — online at top, invited at bottom
  • Less visual overhead
